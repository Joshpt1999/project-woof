Boxer

Boxer Information

An intelligent, loyal pet, the boxer has a high need for companionship and exercise. They are not happy when regularly left home alone. Boxers are intolerant of hot weather, and care must be taken to prevent them from getting overheated.The Boxer is a medium-sized, short-haired breed of dog, developed in Germany. The coat is smooth and tight-fitting; colors are fawn or brindled, with or without white markings, and white.
